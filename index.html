Best PMP answer habit

Read the scenario, identify the approach, then choose the answer that protects value, people, and collaboration before escalating or acting alone.

For Agile/Hybrid scenarios, think: coach → collaborate → make work visible → inspect & adapt.

Quick challenge

A project sponsor requests a major change mid-sprint. What should the PM do first?

Discuss it with the product owner and add it to the product backlog for prioritization. Avoid disrupting a committed sprint unless truly urgent.

Formula lab

Know the idea behind a number, not only the calculation.

Cost Performance Index

CPI = EV ÷ AC

Cost efficiency. Above 1.00 means the project is getting more value than it spends.

Schedule Performance Index

SPI = EV ÷ PV

Schedule efficiency. Above 1.00 means the project is ahead of plan.

Cost Variance

CV = EV − AC

Positive is favorable; negative is over budget.

Schedule Variance

SV = EV − PV

Positive is ahead of schedule; negative is behind.

Estimate at Completion

EAC = BAC ÷ CPI

Use when current cost performance is expected to continue.

To-Complete Performance Index

TCPI = (BAC − EV) ÷ (BAC − AC)

Efficiency required from now on to meet the original budget.
